Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how LatchFit, developed by Zacquie (“we,” “our,” or “us”), collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application and website (latchfit.com).

1. Information We Collect

  • User-Provided Data: Information you voluntarily provide, such as name, email address (if you contact support), and optional profile information.
  • Health & Usage Data: Feeding logs, pumping data, calorie tracking, sleep and diaper logs that you enter into the app. This data stays on your device unless you choose to back it up or share it.
  • Technical Data: Device type, app version, and anonymous analytics (if enabled) to improve performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core app features like tracking feeding, calories, and sleep.
  • To maintain and improve app functionality and user experience.
  • To respond to customer support requests.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored locally on your device by default. If you choose to back up or sync, your data may be processed by third-party services (e.g., Apple iCloud, Google Drive) under their terms. We use reasonable safeguards to protect information, but no system is 100% secure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share limited data only:

  • With service providers (only as needed to operate features you opt into).
  • If required by law or to protect legal rights.

5. Children’s Privacy

LatchFit is intended for use by adults. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

6. Your Choices

You can edit or delete the data you enter in the app at any time. You may also uninstall the app to remove all locally stored information.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by updating the “Effective Date.”
